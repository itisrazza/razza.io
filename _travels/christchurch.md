## Red Zones

After the [2011 Christchurch Earthquake](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2011_Christchurch_earthquake), the city has closed areas deemed unfeasable to rebuild and turned them into parks. These are known as [residential red zones](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Residential_red_zone).
